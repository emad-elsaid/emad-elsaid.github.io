# Red Rising

## Comparisons & Influences

It gave the impression that it's a mix of the following: 

- **The inverse of The Matrix**: instead of the guy going from clean sterile world to the shit hole
  humanity lives in outside of the matrix. in Red Rising Darrow goes the opposite way. 
- **Ender's Game**: Where the kid is "The one" and he's chosen for his innate ability to lead and
  conquer. 
- **The Hunger Games**: Where the society is split into different factions each bred for a specific
  job (or you can draw parallel to Brave New World) 
- **Harry Potter**: Where Darrow joins an institute or a school where it's split to different houses
  and they have to compete for a prize.

## Overall Impression

In General I liked it and it hooked me the first chapter, Writing style is easy to follow and the
choice of words is pretty simple. It's very enjoyable and I would recommend it if you're into
Sci-Fi with a hint on military setup like Enders Game
